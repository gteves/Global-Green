Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy utilizes the heat from beneath the Earth's surface to generate electricity and provide heating solutions. It is a clean and sustainable energy source with minimal environmental impact.

Key Benefits of Geothermal Energy

  • Reliable: Provides a consistent energy output regardless of weather conditions.
  • Eco-Friendly: Produces significantly lower greenhouse gas emissions compared to fossil fuels.
  • Efficient: Geothermal plants have a high efficiency rate and a small land footprint.

Challenges

Despite its advantages, geothermal energy faces challenges such as high initial costs for exploration and installation, and geographical limitations to areas with geothermal activity.
